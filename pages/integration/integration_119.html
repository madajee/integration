Takeaways
  • It appears to me that a dev resource can develop (not deliver) about 18-20 points in a sprint and can target for a velocity of 35 - 40 points as PO/SM drives the PB items. To make things a bit interesting, developer lead can create their own metric of 1-8 points per commit.
  • Though I have used mule VM connectors in POC, we have to follow on architectural and business decisions, enterprise guidelines before we choose over synchrous vs asynchronous styles. It is a bit easier to not worry about creating domain project in studio and one should do the same for their POC.
